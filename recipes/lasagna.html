Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy ricotta cheese. It's a hearty and satisfying dish perfect for a family dinner or a special occasion. The combination of flavors and textures makes it a true crowd-pleaser.

Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables, such as spinach or mushrooms, or by using different types of cheese. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brown ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat. Drain excess grease.
  3. Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain and set aside.
  6. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese and 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread 1/2 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Layer 3 noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Top with half of the remaining meat sauce.
  11. Repeat layers of noodles, ricotta mixture, and meat sauce.
  12. Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  13. Cover and bake for 30 minutes.
  14. Uncover and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until bubbly and cheese is melted.
  15. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
